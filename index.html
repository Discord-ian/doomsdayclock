WHAT DOES THIS MEAN?

THE DOOMSDAY CLOCK WAS CREATED BY THE BULLETIN OF THE ATOMIC SCIENTISTS IN ORDER TO CONVEY THE THREAT OF NUCLEAR WEAPONS. IT HAS CHANGED OVER TIME TO LOOK AT CLIMATE CHANGE, AND OTHER DISRUPTIVE TECHNOLOGIES.

HOW DID WE GET HERE?

OVER TIME, THE DOOMSDAY CLOCK HAS CHANGED. IT WAS THE FURTHEST FROM MIDNIGHT IN 1991 DUE TO THE STRATEGIC ARMS REDUCTION TREATY AND THE COLLAPSE OF THE SOVIET UNION. IT IS CLOSEST TO MIDNIGHT IN 2020 DUE TO INACTION OF WORLD LEADERS ON ISSUES LIKE CLIMATE CHANGE. OTHER EVENTS INCLUDE THE END OF THE INTERMEDIATE-RANGE NUCLEAR FORCES TREATY BETWEEN THE UNITED STATES AND RUSSIA, AS WELL AS INCREASED TENSIONS BETWEEN US AND IRAN.
